### Advanced Cash Flow Exercise

### Additional Complexities
- **Sale of Fixed Assets**: Treatment of gains/losses.
  - Gain → subtract from OCF (not an operating cash flow).
  - Total receipt → Investing CF.
- **Accelerated vs. Straight-line Depreciation**: Cash impact via taxes.
- **Equity Changes**: Stock issuance, buybacks, dividend distribution.
- **Long-term Debt**: New issuances, repayments, conversions.

### Reconciliation
```
Starting Cash
+ Operating CF
+ Investing CF (usually negative)
+ Financing CF
= Ending Cash
```
The ending cash must match the balance sheet figure.

### Common Errors
- ❌ Forgetting to remove gains/losses from OCF.
- ❌ Confusing WC investments (operating) with CapEx (investing).
- ❌ Not considering the depreciation tax shield.
