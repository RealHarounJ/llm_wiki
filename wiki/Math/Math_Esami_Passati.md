## 🔑 Key Observations from All 4 Exams

### Q1 — Study of Function: What They Always Ask
- The function often involves **eˣ combined with something** (polynomial, |abs|, √)
- Always: domain → limits/asymptotes → f'(x) → monotonicity → f''(x) → graph
- **Non-differentiability:** always check at points where √, |abs|, or 1/x appear

### Q2 — Integrals: What They Always Ask
- Integration by parts (log²x, x·eˣ)
- Substitution with radicals (√x)
- Definite integrals / area between curves

### Q3/Q4 — 2-Variable Functions
- Domain: always involves √ or ln → set conditions
- Critical points via Hessian: always the same method
- Level curves: circles/ellipses/lines depending on f

### Q5 — Linear Systems with Parameter
- Always a 2×2 or 3×3 system with one parameter (k, h, a)
- Method: Gaussian elimination → find rank → discuss cases
- Find solutions for each case
