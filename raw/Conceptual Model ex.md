EXERCISE: Draw an ER schema for a DB which will support a beauty center in giving its customers the possibility to purchase products or book treatments. The database will manage data related to suppliers, customers, various types of products, and employees. • For suppliers, we need to save: VAT number, company name, contact details, addresses (a supplier may have more than one address. We also need to know which products are supplied by which suppliers and to save the invoices related to each order. Several products may be contained in a single order. • For customers, we need to save some basic personal information (e.g., name, address, birthdate…). A customer may book a treatment or buy a product. The database should save information about the treatments and the products bought by each customer. • For products, we want to save the name, the price and the available quantity. • For treatments, we need to save information about the name, the price and the employee performing the treatment. There are two main typologies of treatment: body treatments and facial treatments. • For employees, we want to save the name and the specializations (an employee may have many specializations).
